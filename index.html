> ACYD is a Slovak multi-genre experimental rapper, sound engineer, and self-taught producer based in Brno, Czech Republic. Known for his anarchistic, anti-establishment and system-provoking lyrics blended with boundary-pushing sound — ranging from old-school phonk, through electronic avant-garde, to alternative punk. Born 13.12.2000, emerging into the new millennium as the epitome of the ACAB acronym. His lyrics focus on populist, oppressive, neo-fascist and anti-democratic tendencies in Slovak political and social reality.
